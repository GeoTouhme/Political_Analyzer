================================================================================
NOTES:
- Risk is also affected by Sentiment (VADER Engine).
- Term scores are capped at 3x frequency per article to prevent bias.
- GRAY TERMS detect aggression hidden in diplomatic language.
  They OVERRIDE Green term reductions when defiance context is present.
- Negation patterns (will not + treaty verb) should be matched as phrases,
  not as individual words, to avoid false positives.
- ORANGE TERMS capture economic pressure tools used as coercion.
- PURPLE TERMS capture non-traditional and hybrid threat indicators.
- Context matters: some terms (e.g. "sanctions") can appear in both
  coercive and relieving contexts. Prioritize phrase-level matching.
